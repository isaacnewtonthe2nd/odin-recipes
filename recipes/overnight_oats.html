Easy Overnight Oats

Description

This recipe for me is a meal prep breakfast staple. Great for keeping you full throughout the morning before lunch and very easy to make. If you are also physically active like me, it is a good source of protein especially if you're going to be adding some whey protein powder or greek yogurt.

You can make this on the weekend and store them in your fridge for up to 5 days and still retian the delicious taste. I usually make them Sunday evening so it lasts a bit longer.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 2 teaspoons chia seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon or more to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh blueberries

How To Make

  1. Combine milk, yogurt, oats, honey, chia seeds, and cinnamon in a 1/2-pint jar with a lid; cover and shake until combined.
  2. Fold in blueberries
  3. Cover and refrigerate, 8 hours to overnight. Enjoy!
  4. It's that easy!
